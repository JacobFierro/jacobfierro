Music Head - iPhone App

We made this little iPhone app in 2009 mostly for fun, it teaches pitch recognition via the "memory game" and I can tell you from experience that it gets pretty hard. It was a great experience and I earned enough in the app store to pay for my Kindle! Score.

The app was released under the name Oorby Studios.

Visit www.musicheadapp.com.
